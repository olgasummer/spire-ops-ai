The challenge

Spire's OSS stack is hitting its limits.

  • 01
    Cardinality explosion110+ satellites × subsystems × sensors overwhelming InfluxDB OSS compaction and retention.
  • 02
    Burst ingestion bottleneckHigh-volume telemetry bursts during 5–10 min ground pass windows cause write failures.
  • 03
    Manual fleet monitoringStatic Grafana thresholds miss subtle degradation — battery fade, thermal creep, wheel wear.
  • 04
    ML data access blockedOSS retention policies causing data loss that degrades model training quality.
